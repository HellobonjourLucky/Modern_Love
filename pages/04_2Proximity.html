Tinder operates by presenting the profiles of individual users located within the chosen radius in your present area. Employing an algorithm known as trilateration, (Figures 20-21)45 Tinder obtains precise latitude and longitude coordinates for each user.46 This groundbreaking feature has been widely acclaimed as one of the most effective methods for meeting new people.

Moreover, as users are limited to matches in their current location on Tinder, they may exhaust potential matches in their city without much success. In such cases, I observed some users changed their Tinder location to explore better places for potential matches.

Apart from exploring romantic relationships, many individuals use Tinder to form friendships or professional networks.
